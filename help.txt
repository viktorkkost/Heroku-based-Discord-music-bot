.help - list of commands

.play - play a youtube video/url/playlist in a voice channel
.dc - disconnect from the current voice channel
.fs - skip the current song
.shuffle - shuffle the queue
.np - show the song which is playing now
.q - show the song queue
.clearq - clear the song queue
.remove - remove a specific entry from the queue
.s - search for a youtube video and post the found link

.hello - say hello to the bot
.kill - stop the bot
